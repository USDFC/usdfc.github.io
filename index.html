The Digital Forensics Consortium (DFC) is a 501 3(c) non-profit organization created to educate students, private sector, and government organizations about digtial forensics and promote its value. We leverage our vast intellect of our Board of Directors - comprised of some of the industry's foremost experts - to mature and invest in the future of this critical capability. Through our challenge programs, we strive to inspire and invest in our Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) communities with academia and industry.
